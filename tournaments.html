Tournament History

Border Wars – Fall 2024

We hosted our first ever Border Wars event against the University of Wyoming at Colorado State University. It was a tough match and although we gave it our all, Wyoming came out ahead. The event was an amazing experience and marked the beginning of a competitive rivalry and friendship between both teams.

NACE – Spring 2025

In Spring 2025, we participated in the NACE open bracket. Our team finished 3rd in our group stage, with Wyoming once again taking first place. The matches were intense and showed how far we've come as a team in a short time. Even without visuals, the progress was clear — we're building momentum for future wins.

Border Wars – Spring 2025

We traveled to the University of Wyoming for the Spring 2025 rematch of Border Wars. Despite another tough loss, our team’s communication, structure, and confidence continue to improve. We’re proud of the effort and excited for what lies ahead.
