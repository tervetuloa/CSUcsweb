How to Join

We're always looking for skilled and dedicated players to join our team. Tryouts are held at the beginning of each semester. Stay tuned to our Discord and social channels for updates on registration and eligibility requirements.

  • Must be a currently enrolled CSU student
  • Prior experience with CS:GO or CS2 preferred
  • Teamwork and communication skills are essential
