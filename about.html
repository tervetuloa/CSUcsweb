About CSU Counter-Strike 2

We are a competitive team formed by students at Colorado State University. Our goal is to excel in esports through teamwork, strategy, and dedication.

We participate in collegiate leagues, scrims, and tournaments throughout the academic year. Our members bring diverse experience and a shared passion for competitive gaming.
