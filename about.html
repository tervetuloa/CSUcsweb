About CSU Counter-Strike 2

Founded by a group of passionate students, the CSU CS2 team competes in collegiate leagues across the U.S., demonstrating precision, teamwork, and sportsmanship. Our mission is to foster competitive excellence while supporting each other in and out of the game.
